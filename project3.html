The Holy Bible of Homosexuality

Senior Thesis Project Publication

The Neue Haas Grotesk Font Book is a detailed exploration of the typeface’s design, history, and visual application. This project presents a structured study of Neue Haas Grotesk, showcasing its typographic characteristics through carefully composed layouts. Emphasizing hierarchy, spacing, and contrast, the book serves as both an analytical and visual reference for the typeface, demonstrating its adaptability and impact in contemporary design.
