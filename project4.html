Ketchup App Startup: Founding UI/UX Design


As the founding UI/UX designer for Ketchup, a dynamic app startup, I played a pivotal role in shaping the initial user experience and visual identity. From the ground up, I was responsible for crafting the app's interface, ensuring it was both intuitive and engaging for early adopters.

Key Contributions:


  • Initial Wireframing & Prototyping: Developed the foundational structure and user flow, translating the startup's vision into tangible design concepts.
  • Visual Design & Branding: Created a cohesive visual language, including color palettes, typography, and iconography, to establish a distinct brand presence.
  • User Testing & Iteration: Conducted early-stage user testing to gather feedback and refine the app's design, ensuring a user-centric approach.
  • Collaboration & Communication: Worked closely with the founding team to align design with business goals and technical feasibility.

This project was a unique opportunity to build a product from its inception, navigating the challenges and excitement of a startup environment. My work on Ketchup laid the groundwork for its early success, emphasizing the importance of user-focused design in building a strong foundation.
