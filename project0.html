The RealReal Web Banners


For a Visual Communication class I took in Fall 2023, I had to create an array of web banners for a local San Francisco-based company. I picked The RealReal, a luxury reseller that emphasizes sustainability and circular fashion.


This project taught me a lot about balancing aesthetics with branding. The challenge was to make sure the banners felt high-end but also approachable. I played with typography, negative space, and product imagery to craft a campaign that reflected The RealReal’s identity beyond just designer clothing—luxury as a lifestyle.


The final ad concept revolved around the idea that luxury is more than just material goods; it’s an ethos of sustainability and timeless value.
